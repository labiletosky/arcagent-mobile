How it works
🔗
01
Connect Wallet
Connect Rabby or MetaMask. Switch to Arc Testnet automatically.
📦
02
Place Order
Enter item, amount, and receiver address. USDC locks in the contract.
🤖
03
Agent Executes
Approved agent confirms delivery and triggers on-chain payment release.
✅
04
Payment Settles
Funds go directly to the receiver. No bank, no waiting, no cut.
Why ArcAgent
🔒
Trustless Escrow
Payment locks in the smart contract. Neither party can touch it until conditions are met.
↩️
Auto Refund
If the agent doesn't execute within 24 hours, buyers can claim a full refund automatically.
📬
Any Receiver
Specify any wallet address as the payment receiver. Pay freelancers, creators, or AI agents.
💵
USDC Native
Built on Arc where USDC is the gas token. Stable, predictable fees every time.
⚡
Sub-Second Finality
Transactions confirm in under a second on Arc. No waiting for block confirmations.
🌐
Public API
Query orders, balances, and stats programmatically. Open infrastructure anyone can build on.
